FAQs

What can i order from SuperGenie?

Anything you want. If it's possible and legal, just give us a try ;)

How much does this cost?

SuperGenie serves you for FREE, you pay for what you order though.

How quickly will I get once I request it?

As soon as possible. We work with several partners to expedite the delivery process. Having said that, we are always a chat away to help you anytime.

Where is SuperGenie available?

SuperGenie serves all of India. If you live outside and want to order/get something done for your beloved ones, that's possible too.

How do you charge me?

When the order has been finalized, we send you a secure link that you can use to complete the payment via NetBanking/Credit/Debit card, we Don't store any of your card details. In case you choose COD as an option, you can pay at the time of delivery.

What are SuperGenie's work hours?

SuperGenie needs rest to regain its energy and serve you better. SuperGenie works for you tirelessly from 9AM to 11PM everyday.

How do you know where to deliver?

Once we finalize the order, we request your email or phone number(sms) to deliver digital orders(bus, movie tickets etc.) In case of physical orders, we would ask your name, address, if required. This info is only required for the first time. After that, SuperGenie knows who you are and everything is automated, isn't that cool.
